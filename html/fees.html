Submission Guidelines

Authors are requested to send original papers in MS Word (Word 2003 and above) format with Double Space, Times Roman Font (see manuscript guidelines). The papers should have an Abstract of not more than 500 words with three to five keywords related to main theme of the paper and the full paper should be within 3,000 words including tables, graphs and figures. Each paper should contain a title page specifying title of the paper, full name, affiliation, telephone/ fax number and e-mail address of the author (s). The papers must be dispatched through e-mail to iccitm2020@gmail.com and received before the prescribed deadline. Authors of all papers accepted for the conference will be required to give a copyright declaration in a format to be intimated later.
